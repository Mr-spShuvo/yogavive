Lotus Pose (Padmasana)

Padmasana has numerous health benefits for mentality, physicality and spiritual energy. It helps increase circulation in the lower spine, energize and tone abdominal muscles and organs.

Plank Pose (Kumbhakasana)

Considered one of the best moves for core strength, Plank Pose strengthens your abdominals and promotes stability.

Upward-Facing Dog (Urdhva Mukha Svanasana)

Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

This classic pose opens your shoulders, lengthens your spine, and stretches your hamstrings. Since your head is below your heart, the mild inversion creates a calming effect.

Warrior I Pose (Virabadhrasana I)

This energizing pose strengthens your legs, arms, and back muscles. It also gives your chest, shoulders, neck, thighs, and ankles a nice stretch.

Marichi's Pose III (Marichyasana III)

The benefits of Marichyasana 3 include massaging your main internal organs, aiding with digestion, spinal mobility, chest opening.

Garland Pose (Malasana)

Malasana stretches the thighs, groin, hips, ankles, and torso. It tones the abdominal muscles and improves the function of the colon to help with elimination.
